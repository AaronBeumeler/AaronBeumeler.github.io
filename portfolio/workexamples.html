This example shows a graphical representation of the database. Included in this diagram are entities, their relationships and attributes. Math Game

I created this game to help my daughter study for her weekly tests over multiplication facts. In the tests she was required to answer twenty random questions correctly under one minute. This game is my attempt to make studying for her tests fun!

How It Works

The Math Jungle lets players select a multiple to practice, then a time limit. They attempt to answer twenty questions correctly, collecting gems and improving on their stats along the way. After the player has answered twenty multiplication questions or time has expired, statistics are displayed in the left of the panel. Scrolling down in that area allows them to review their answers from the previous round.
